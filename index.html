Automatic Approval Routing

Amount determines who approves — no one has to decide manually

Under ₱10,000
Finance Officer only — Jennifer reviews and closes the request (48h SLA)
₱10,000 – ₱99,999
Finance Officer → Finance Head (Cindy) — two-stage approval required (48h each)
₱100,000 and above
Finance Officer → Finance Head → CFO approval — full three-stage sign-off (72h SLA at CFO)
